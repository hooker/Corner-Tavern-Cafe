(Pittsburgh, PA)

Wigle Distillery is a family owned grain-to-bottle distillery named after Phillip Wigle. Wigle, a PA distilling pioneer, defended his right to distill in a tussle with a tax collector and unwittingly helped spark the Whiskey Rebellion, which pitted Western Pennsylvania distillers against George Washington's troops. He was sentenced to hang for treason but was ultimately pardoned by Washington.

Wigle Organic Rye

Paying tribute to the original American whiskey mash, Wigle uses Organic Monongahela Rye, which has grown throughout western PA, including the river valley surrounding Pittsburg for years

(Charlottesville, VA)

Ragged Branch is a grain-to-bottle distillery in Charlottesville VA. Ragged Branch and Whistle Pig share the same master distiller, Dave Pickerell, who recently passed in November of 2018. Unlike Whistle Pig, Ragged Branch gave Pickerell the resources to distill all spirits, rather than source whiskeys as Whistle Pig does.
