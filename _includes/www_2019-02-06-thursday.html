(Purcelville, VA)

Catoctin Creek Distilling is quite possibly the most awarded distillery in Virginia, sourcing local grains and ingredients for their grain-to-bottle production. The name comes from the picturesque creek that flows by the distillery, the same that flows through Frederick County MD.
